Project 02 — Privacy-First Mobile Platform

Graphite OS

Surveillance is the default. We rejected it. Graphite OS is a hardened, privacy-first mobile operating system built on the premise that your device should work for you — not the companies selling access to your attention. Clean. Armored. Uncompromising.

Core Pillars
01

Zero Surveillance

No telemetry. No tracking. No silent data collection feeding someone else's empire. Every packet that leaves your device is one you've deliberately sent. Your digital life is yours to govern — not theirs to mine.

02

Hardened Android

Built on a stripped and re-engineered Android base with attack surface reduction, memory-safe implementations, and verified boot at its core. Security isn't a feature bolted on — it's the substrate everything is built upon.

03

Total Control

Permission architecture designed by principle, not by convenience. You decide what runs, when it runs, and what it touches. A permission model that defaults to refusal and requires your explicit, informed consent to extend trust.

04

Clean by Design

No bloatware. No duplicate apps. No manufacturer overlays feeding dark patterns into your daily use. A system that respects your cognitive space as much as it respects your privacy — fast, minimal, and brutally focused.

"Privacy is not a feature. It is the only acceptable default. Everything else is a concession someone else decided for you."

— Graphite OS Design Principle
